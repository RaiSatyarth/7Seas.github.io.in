History

Global Recognition & Expansion

7Seas was awarded the prestigious "World's Best Luxury Beach Resort" by International Hospitality Awards, marking our place among the elite in global hospitality. We expanded to the Maldives and Bali, bringing our signature blend of Indian hospitality and global luxury standards to new shores.

This year also saw the launch of our 7Seas Foundation, committing 5% of all profits to ocean conservation and coastal community development, proving that luxury and sustainability can go hand in hand.

Rising From Challenges

After surviving the devastating Mumbai floods that damaged our property, we emerged stronger with a complete renovation that incorporated flood-resistant design while enhancing the guest experience. This period tested our resilience but ultimately led to innovations in sustainable luxury that became industry benchmarks.

Our "Phoenix Package" offered during the reopening became legendary, symbolizing our ability to transform challenges into opportunities for excellence.

Humble Beginnings

Founded during the global financial crisis by three friends with a shared dream, 7Seas started as a modest 12-room guesthouse overlooking Juhu Beach. Against all odds, we focused on exceptional service and authentic experiences rather than extravagant decor.

Our first breakthrough came when a famous film director stayed with us and recommended 7Seas to his celebrity friends, putting us on the map as Mumbai's best-kept secret. This taught us the power of genuine connections and word-of-mouth in hospitality.
